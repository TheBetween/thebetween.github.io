the between

The between of your personal context and current state is here symbolized by temperament (a more fixed aspect of you) and emotion (a more changing aspect of you), although context could also include personal history, culture, and learned defenses; current state could also include bodily state and immediate happenings.

This tool is designed to help you consider your current emotional state, to help you share your emotional self with another, and to visualize the between: it is in that space that we are presently defined.

The between of you and another = the function of [the function of (your personal context)(your current state)][the function of (their personal context)(their current state)]

"There are no things; there are only relationships."

-Sir Iain McGilchrist
